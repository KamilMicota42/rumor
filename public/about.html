Rumor.

We believe in free speech and your right to communicate with people privately. We don’t read your messages, because we can’t. They are encrypted on your device. After you leave a room we don’t even know if you've ever been there. When you delete your account there is nothing left about you in our database.

What we know:

  • Your username
  • Time when you send a message
  • Room where you send a message
  • What rooms you are in

What we don’t know:

  • Content of your message
  • Your password and decryption keys
  • How many accounts do you have
  • All from left after you leave a room or delete your account.

We would like to know less about you, but for now if you want to be extra private it’s a good practice to:

  • Use a random username and multiple accounts.
  • If you have some important data to share create a new room and leave it after the conversation.
  • Logout if anyone else has access to your computer.
